Faq

When did you open?

We opened our flagship location in May 2010 at 12th and Sansom St., Philadelphia.

What kind of bread do you use?

We offer seeded, unseeded or wheat rolls from Carangi Bakery, which is located in the heart of South Philadelphia. We feel Carangi Bakery offers the best quality roll for our sandwiches. All rolls are delivered fresh everyday!

What if I don’t want bread (and I have a gluten allergy)?

No problem, we offer all our sandwiches in bowls (no bread). You can choose from a naked, salad or haystack bowl. The haystack is any sandwich on top of a bowl of fries.

Do you slow cook all your meats?

Hell yeah! We slow cook all our meats daily…pulled pork, brisket, chicken & turkeys. We strive on sourcing top quality meats and always improving our recipes.

Do you cater?

Yes, we cater all size groups from ten people all the way up to an entire office lunch, birthday party, etc. For more details, click here or call (215) 922-0102.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes, well sell gift cards in-store only. Stop by the store during normal business hours and you can purchase any amount gift cards. If you can’t make to the store, call or email us, and someone will get in touch with you about sending it via mail.

Do you sell t-shirts?

Yes, order online or stop by the store during normal business hours and you can purchase the LOVE Sriracha, Baconista or Pig t-shirt for $15. Sizes available are small, medium, large or extra large.

Do you sell whole pigs?

Sorry, we do not sell whole pigs, but we will be more than happy to cater you a make your own pulled pork platter or sandwich stations. For more details, call (215) 922-0102.

Do you take reservations?

No, all seating is first come, first serve. Don’t worry we have plenty of dinning room tables and counter seats, all together it’s a mixture of 40 table and counter seats.

What is you delivery range?

We deliver up to five blocks. For large group or catering orders please call the store for more details, (215) 922-0102.
